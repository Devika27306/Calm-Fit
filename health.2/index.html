What is Fitness?

Fitness is the ability to perform daily activities with vigor while reducing the risk of health problems. It includes strength, endurance, flexibility, and mental well-being.

Definition of Fitness

Fitness is the capability to execute physical tasks efficiently without excessive fatigue. It encompasses both physical health and mental resilience.

Key Components of Fitness

  • Cardiovascular Endurance: Improves heart and lung health.
  • Muscular Strength: Enhances the ability to lift and carry weight.
  • Muscular Endurance: Boosts stamina for prolonged activities.
  • Flexibility: Prevents injuries by improving joint mobility.
  • Body Composition: Maintains a healthy fat-to-muscle ratio.
